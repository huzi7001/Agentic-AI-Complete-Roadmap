## How to Use This Roadmap

- I will follow this roadmap **month by month**.
- Each month has:
  - a **goal**
  - **topics**
  - **projects**
  - **deliverables**
  - **checklist**
- I will complete the month only after:
  - understanding the concepts,
  - writing code,
  - pushing projects to GitHub,
  - updating progress here.

## Learning Strategy

I am following a **Pareto Principle (80/20)** strategy.

That means:

- focus first on the most important concepts,
- build real projects early,
- avoid wasting time on low-value theory,
- keep improving through practice.

2. **Machine Learning**
